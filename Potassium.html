Potassium


This element is found primarily inside the cells of the body. Low levels in the blood may indicate severe diarrhea, alcoholism, or excessive use of water pills. Low potassium levels can cause muscle weakness and heart problems.


If you would like to know more about Potassium read on

Common Causes of Potassium Increase 

Adrenal hypo-function, cortisol resistance, acidosis, ongoing tissue destruction

Common Causes of Potassium Decrease

Diarrhea, diuretic use, kidney problems, adrenal hyperfunction
